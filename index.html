BizCraft!

At BizCraft we understand the hassle to find the perfect server. Lucky Enough, we strive to have the best gameplay to offer to all of our players. Our server is completed with professional and fair staff who are highy experienced in minecraft and will be able to help you in all aspects of minecraft. BizCraft is filled with many different features to enhance your gameplay, creating the perfect server to spend your countless minecraft hours on.


What we offer our users...

Numerous Plugins

BizCraft is a survival based factions server loaded with many plugins such as, Factions, MCMMO, Playerheads, Silkspawners, Essentials, Auctions, No Cheat and more. Our Plugins are specifially loaded to improve your in-game experience.

Dedicated Management Team

Our Management is an experienced and motivated handful of people that manage and maintain the server on a daily-basis. Our Management team has one goal, that is to provide our user base with the best possible server on the net, No excuses.

99.5% Uptime Guarantee

At BizCraft we guarantee all of our users a game experience to remember, in doing that we strive highly offer our user base a solid 99.5% uptime rate. Meaning server downtime will be rare, if unexpected so our users can play our server freely.

Donations Shop

Want a kickstart to your game? Want additional commands, tools and permissions? Donating is the way to go. Donating to BizCraft gets you additional gameplay features, thus enhancing your in-game experience. All donations recieved go towards monthly server bills.

DDoS Protection

BizCraft's servers are hosted on a DDoS protected network helping to ensure all of our players the maximum uptime guarantee offering you a smooth and reliable server to play on with your mates
